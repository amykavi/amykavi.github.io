This page is for our projects in AP Computer Science Principles


This is a game I created in Scratch! It is a chase game (using the arrow keys) in which you must help Space Doggo eat donuts while avoiding the dinosaur. Once the dog sprite touches the donut, a new donut appears in a random location. The Dinocaur continously glides to random locations on the screen, progressively going faster every 10 seconds.

This is a quiz app created in MIT App Inventor. It is a fill in the blank vine quiz. Each of the 10 questions has a photo of a popular vine as well as a quote from the vine. Using the screen's keynpard, you must type in the correct word in the blank.
